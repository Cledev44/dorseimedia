About Dorsei Media

At Dorsei Media, our story is one of passion, innovation, and faith. Founded by Prince Osei Boateng with a God-given gift for electronics and media technology, Dorsei Media has grown into a trusted brand serving TV stations, radio networks, churches, and businesses throughout Ghana and beyond.

Our Story

Growing up, Prince Osei Boateng always carried an unusual passion that set him apart. From an early age, he was fascinated by electronics. While other children played with toys, he spent his days moving from one refuse dump to another, scavenging for faulty radios and abandoned electronic parts - not for treasures in the ordinary sense, but for components he could bring back to life.

This passion led him to enroll at Abetifi Technical Institute in the Eastern Region of Ghana in 2012, where he studied Electronics Engineering Technology. Later, he pursued Sound Engineering at Eco Sound Engineering School, one of the best in the field.

Though opportunities to work in a station didn't immediately appear, Prince stayed faithful to the journey. He began offering his services to churches, fixing their sound systems and installing equipment. A turning point came when he met a man of God who introduced him to his brother, who was setting up a TV station. Prince was offered the chance to volunteer as a technician handling sound for live and recorded shows, marking the true beginning of his media career.

The name "Dorsei Media" was born from a chance encounter. After taking pictures for a young lady named Doreen, she jokingly suggested combining their names: "Osei and Doreen, Dorsei Media!" The idea resurfaced later, and Prince registered the company under this name.

Today, by the grace of God, Dorsei Media stands strong with over six years of professional experience. What started as a passion among broken radios has become a trusted brand, helping others bring their visions to life through innovation, excellence, and faith.
